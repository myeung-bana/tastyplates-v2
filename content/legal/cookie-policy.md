# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** January 6, 2025

## Introduction

TastyPlates ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

- User authentication and session management
- Security features
- Basic website navigation

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for the website to work.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features:

- Language preferences
- Location settings
- Display preferences

### Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

- Page views and navigation patterns
- Time spent on pages
- Error tracking
- Performance metrics

We use these cookies to improve our website's functionality and user experience.

### Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness:

- Ad personalization
- Conversion tracking
- Social media integration

## Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device:

- **Google Analytics:** Website analytics and usage statistics
- **Firebase:** Authentication and backend services
- **Social Media Platforms:** Share buttons and embedded content (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)
- **Upstash Redis:** Session management and caching

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

## How We Use Cookies

### Authentication and Security

- Store your login session
- Remember your authentication state
- Protect against unauthorized access

### Personalization

- Remember your language preferences
- Save your location settings
- Customize content based on your preferences

### Analytics

- Track how you use our website
- Understand which features are most popular
- Identify areas for improvement

### Performance

- Cache content for faster loading
- Optimize server response times
- Reduce bandwidth usage

## Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

### Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

- View and delete cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)
- Set preferences for third-party cookies

### Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you may see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your cookie preferences

### Third-Party Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain third-party cookies:

- **Google Analytics:** Use the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Social Media:** Adjust your privacy settings on each platform

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not work properly:

- You may need to log in more frequently
- Your preferences may not be saved
- Some personalized features may be unavailable
- Website performance may be affected

## Cookie Duration

### Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session while browsing.

### Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences between visits.
