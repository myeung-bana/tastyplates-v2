# Content Guidelines

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

Welcome to TastyPlates! These Content Guidelines help ensure our community remains helpful, respectful, and authentic. By posting content on TastyPlates, you agree to follow these guidelines.

## Core Principles

### 1. Be Authentic
- Write reviews based on your genuine experiences
- Post your own photos (no stock images)
- Disclose any conflicts of interest
- Don't post fake or misleading reviews

### 2. Be Respectful
- Treat others with courtesy and respect
- Avoid personal attacks or harassment
- Respect different opinions and preferences
- Be constructive in your criticism

### 3. Be Helpful
- Provide specific, detailed feedback
- Include useful information for other diners
- Update reviews if circumstances change
- Answer questions from other users

## What You Can Post

### Restaurant Reviews
✅ **Allowed:**
- Honest opinions about your dining experience
- Details about food quality, service, and ambiance
- Information about menu items, pricing, and portions
- Photos of your meals and the restaurant
- Tips and recommendations for other diners

❌ **Not Allowed:**
- Reviews of restaurants you haven't visited
- Multiple reviews of the same visit
- Reviews based solely on delivery or takeout (mark accordingly)
- Reviews that violate these guidelines

### Photos
✅ **Allowed:**
- Photos of food, drinks, and dishes
- Restaurant interior and exterior shots
- Menu photos
- Photos of the dining experience

❌ **Not Allowed:**
- Stock photos or images from other sources
- Photos with offensive or inappropriate content
- Photos that invade others' privacy
- Heavily edited or misleading photos
- Photos containing personal information

### Comments and Replies
✅ **Allowed:**
- Thoughtful responses to reviews
- Questions about experiences
- Additional tips and information
- Constructive discussions

❌ **Not Allowed:**
- Spam or promotional content
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Off-topic comments
- Attempts to manipulate ratings

## Prohibited Content

### Illegal Content
- Content that violates local, state, or federal laws
- Copyright or trademark infringement
- Content promoting illegal activities
- Content that endangers minors

### Harmful Content
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Threats of violence or harm
- Harassment or bullying
- Sexually explicit content
- Graphic violence

### Misleading Content
- Fake reviews or ratings
- Impersonation of others
- Deceptive or fraudulent content
- Manipulation of ratings or rankings

### Spam and Commercial Content
- Unsolicited advertising or promotions
- Affiliate links without disclosure
- Repetitive or irrelevant content
- Mass-posting or automated posting

### Conflicts of Interest
- Restaurant owners reviewing their own establishments
- Employees reviewing their workplace (without disclosure)
- Competitors posting negative reviews
- Paid reviews (without disclosure)

## Restaurant-Specific Guidelines

### Restaurant Owners
✅ **You Can:**
- Respond professionally to reviews
- Correct factual errors politely
- Thank customers for positive feedback
- Update your restaurant information

❌ **You Cannot:**
- Post fake positive reviews
- Ask for review removal (unless it violates guidelines)
- Threaten or harass reviewers
- Offer incentives for positive reviews

### Reviewers
✅ **You Should:**
- Be fair and balanced in your assessment
- Focus on the food, service, and experience
- Mention both positives and negatives
- Update reviews if issues are resolved

❌ **You Shouldn't:**
- Use reviews to extort businesses
- Post based on personal vendettas
- Review based on single negative aspects
- Post during disputes with the restaurant

## Rating Guidelines

### Star Ratings
- **5 Stars:** Exceptional experience, highly recommend
- **4 Stars:** Very good experience, recommend
- **3 Stars:** Good experience with some issues
- **2 Stars:** Below average experience
- **1 Star:** Poor experience, significant problems

### Rating Criteria
Consider these factors when rating:
- Food quality and taste
- Service quality and attentiveness
- Atmosphere and ambiance
- Value for money
- Cleanliness and hygiene

## Photo Guidelines

### Quality Standards
- Clear and well-lit photos
- In-focus images
- Accurate representation of the food/restaurant
- Appropriate framing and composition

### What to Avoid
- Blurry or dark photos
- Photos taken from other sources
- Over-edited or filtered photos
- Photos with personal information visible
- Photos that invade others' privacy

## Privacy and Personal Information

### Protect Your Privacy
- Don't share personal contact information publicly
- Be cautious about revealing your identity
- Control your profile visibility settings

### Respect Others' Privacy
- Don't post photos of people without permission
- Don't share others' personal information
- Respect private conversations
- Don't screenshot and share private messages

## Reporting Violations

### How to Report
If you see content that violates these guidelines:
1. Click the "Report" button on the content
2. Select the reason for reporting
3. Provide additional context if needed
4. Submit the report

### What Happens Next
- Our moderation team reviews all reports
- Action is taken within 24-48 hours
- Reporters are notified of the outcome
- Appeals can be submitted if you disagree

## Consequences of Violations

### First Violation
- Warning and content removal
- Explanation of the violation
- Educational resources provided

### Repeated Violations
- Temporary account suspension
- Content removal
- Feature restrictions

### Serious or Repeated Violations
- Permanent account termination
- Content deletion
- Potential legal action

## Tips for Great Reviews

### Structure Your Review
1. **Start with a summary:** Brief overview of your experience
2. **Describe the food:** What you ordered and how it was
3. **Mention the service:** How staff treated you
4. **Note the atmosphere:** Ambiance and setting
5. **Conclude with recommendations:** Would you return? What to order?

### Be Specific
- Instead of "good food," say "perfectly cooked medium-rare steak"
- Instead of "bad service," say "waited 30 minutes for our order"
- Include dish names, prices, and timing when relevant

### Add Context
- Mention when you visited (time of day, day of week)
- Note if it was busy or quiet
- Mention special occasions or circumstances
- Include dietary requirements if relevant

### Use Helpful Language
- Be descriptive: "crispy," "tender," "flavorful"
- Be balanced: mention pros and cons
- Be constructive: suggest improvements
- Be fair: consider the restaurant's category and price point
